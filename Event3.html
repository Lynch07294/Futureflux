Extempore

About the Event...

"Technical extempore" refers to giving a technical speech or presentation on a topic without prior preparation or notes.
It tests your ability to quickly grasp and convey complex information.

Event Date :- 5 April 2025

Time :- 10 AM to 5 PM

Venue:- Conference hall

Activity type:- Individual

In this event all participant will be given picture at venue after program starts.
Short time for thinking will be given. After the observation time ends, you have to write about the topic
on the page which will be given to you.To excel in extempore speaking, prepare by researching common
topics, practice speaking on the spot, and maintain a natural, confident delivery.

Why to participate?

  • Extempore allows students to think on their feet and outside of the box.
  • It's a fantastic way to hone your communication and time management abilities.
  • Encourages one to think of and develop ideas without any prior planning.
  • It forces students to deal with and analyse the problem at the moment.
